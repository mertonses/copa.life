# Copa Life — App Store English

**Name:** Copa Life
**Subtitle:** A new squad every run  
**Category:** Games / Sports / Strategy

Roll, evaluate your options, and build a starting eleven on a limited budget. Each run combines a changing player pool, chairman decisions, and match scenarios on the road to a national cup.

- Six countries and a changing pool of thousands of players
- Decision-focused profiles using six original copa.life game dimensions
- Formation, chemistry, budget, and chairman expectations
- Live Final Sim, penalties, and a readable match replay
- Optional, moderated Ghost Club opponents
- No ads, in-app purchases, or paid random rewards

copa.life is an independent football strategy game and is not officially endorsed by any club, competition, or athlete.
